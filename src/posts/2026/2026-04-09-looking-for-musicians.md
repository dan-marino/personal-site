# Looking for Bandmates

<p>Just looking to get back into playing with other folks again. Been spending most of my time on guitar, but also very comfortable as a bassist. Pre-covid, I played shows around the city and went on a handful of 1-2 DIY week tours to the midwest and south. Wish I kept track of all the shows, but I've probably performed somewhere in the 50-100 range.</p>

## Audio

<p>I graduated from the Sonic Arts Center at CCNY with a BFA in Audio Engineering and have lots of experience recording in studios, basements and live venues. Logic is my preferred DAW. Been recording a bit with the Zoom L12 to get out of the box a little.</p>

## Goals

<p>Form a group that held weekly rehearsal, performed a local show every month, and record at least one or two songs. Willing to keep it rolling if it's feeling good and natural.</p>

<p>Also just down to jam on some covers.</p>
